About.

VALICO means ‘pathway’ in Latin, which reflects our will to become a new pathway to art and technology.

We aspire to lead the innovations and present new ideas using the strength of the blockchain technology, and plan to introduce an art service that will have a positive effect on cultural ecosystem, with the application of our own technology.

Our Mission is
to promote the values of art and culture.

Core Value: 3C

  • COMMUNICATE

    Good communication is the most important element when working together. We hope to create a corporate culture that can promote precise communications without unnecessary procedures and titles.

  • CURIOSITY

    When in question, we actively search for answers without hesitation, always keeping an open mind for new information.

  • CREATIVITY

    Sometimes, wild imaginations and experiences in other areas become grounds for great ideas. Thus, we encourage our team members to get diverse experiences.
