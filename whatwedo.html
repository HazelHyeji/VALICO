What we do.

  • B.C
    Blockchain

    We are developing VIA CHAIN, our own private blockchain technology specialized in contracts and trades. While existing blockchain technologies focused on fast transactions and creating large amount of blocks, VIA CHAIN focuses on prompt generation of finalized blocks, which reflects fast data processing.

    Also, despite it being run on limited amount of nodes as a private blockchain, it requires minimal resources for the operation, enabling us to maximize the benefits of being private.

    We aim to follow the blockchain standards in our technology. Thus we maximize the utilization of our development resources without creating high costs, which also enables us to easily support the development of services.

  • A.D
    Art Domain

    We support creators with their creative activities, as well as authentications and trades of their work.

    We aim to utilize our genuine blockchain technology to handle essential information and create an ecosystem in and of itself.

    • Archiving service for creators for their authentication
    • A trading platform to change the perception and the possession of artwork
    • Expansion of the grounds for art business regarding exhibitions and trades
    • Creation of an ecosystem utilizing genuine blockchain technology without cryptocurrency
