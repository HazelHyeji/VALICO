What we do.

  • B.C
    Blockchain

    We are developing our own private block chain technology called VIA CHAIN, specializing in contracts and transactions.

    If the existing blockchain technology focuses on fast transactions and large block generation, VIA CHAIN focuses on the finalized block, or faster information processing.

    In addition, due to the nature of the private blockchain, even though it is operated by a limited number of nodes, the resource consumption required for system operation is very small, and the advantage of private can be fully exploited.

    By adopting standard technology, we are maximizing the use of development resources in a cost-free way, and we are making it easier to support service development.

  • A.D
    Art Domain

    Applying VIA CHAIN, we support creators’ activities and work verification and trading. We have the goal of dealing with key information through pure blockchain technology and forming it into a single ecosystem. Business Area

    • Archiving service for creator’s proof of work
    • Trading platform for recognition and ownership of creative products
    • Expanding art business base for exhibition and sale
    • Formation of ecosystems using pure blockchain technology without coin
