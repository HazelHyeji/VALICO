VALICO B.C. is faithful to the basic concept of blockchain, and develops ‘VIA CHAIN,’ its own blockchain protocol that guarantees easy development.

Why VIA CHAIN?

  • A blockchain system suited for business

    It enables the design of protocols required in business such as various types of data and calculations.

  • Focused on prompt generation of finalized blocks

    By enabling finalized blocks to be used promptly, we improved the calculation speed of blocks.

    ※ VIA CHAIN Test specification : 4core 4GB(cloud VM), 3 Node
  • Maximization of expandability by using standard technology

    While the nodes for VIA CHAIN is developed in Java, it enables application of various languages for its API, such as C++, C#, and python.

  • Linked connections between block data

    It enables fast authentications and generation of information upon request of external APIs.
